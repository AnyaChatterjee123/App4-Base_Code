Schizophrenia or Bipolar disorder?

Many people facing challenges in their mental health want to find out what specific illness they are facing in order to be aware of the appropriate and most effective treatments. Bipolar disorder is commonly seen as schizophrenia and vice versa by many, and the misdiagnosis of bipolar disorder and schizophrenia occurs frequently. The symptoms of these two mental illnesses are distinct, so answer a few short questions to find out which one you may have.
